# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

- Be respectful and direct.
- Prefer evidence over posturing.
- Critique ideas, code, and assumptions, not people.
- Keep collaboration useful for operators, contributors, and maintainers.

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment or intimidation
- doxxing or sharing private information
- abusive or discriminatory language
- deliberate disruption of discussion or review

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate these standards and may restrict further participation when needed to protect the project.
